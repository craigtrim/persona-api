# Green Ember Characters - Big Five Personality Reference

Based on character behavior in S.D. Smith's *Green Ember* series.  
Scoring system: [botprofile.work](https://botprofile.work/)

## Scale

| Score | Level     |
|-------|-----------|
| 1     | Very Low  |
| 2     | Low       |
| 3     | Medium    |
| 4     | High      |
| 5     | Very High |

## Trait Key

- **A** = Agreeableness
- **C** = Conscientiousness
- **E** = Extraversion
- **N** = Neuroticism
- **O** = Openness

---

## Protagonists

### Heather Longtreader
**A4 C4 E3 N3 O4**

Compassionate healer-in-training, dedicated and curious. Worries but copes well. Trains in medicine at Cloud Mountain.

### Picket Longtreader
**A2 C4 E2 N5 O3**

Disciplined fighter who struggles with anger and resentment. Inward-focused, emotionally volatile. Grows through the series.

### Smalls (Prince Jupiter)
**A5 C4 E3 N2 O4**

Servant-hearted, humble, extraordinarily kind. Duty-bound heir who carries heavy burden with steadiness. Wise big-picture thinker.

### Helmer
**A2 C5 E2 N2 O2**

Gruff blademaster, extremely disciplined. Emotionally reserved mentor. Traditional, focused, stoic under pressure.

### Lord Rake
**A3 C5 E4 N2 O3**

Tough but fair strategic leader. Charismatic, inspires through action. Steady under fire, practical strategist.

### Emma
**A5 C4 E3 N2 O3**

Nurturing, warm, reliable. Emotionally stable and calming presence. Not attention-seeking but warmly present.

### Kyle
**A4 C2 E4 N3 O4**

Friendly and eager young rabbit. Impulsive, sometimes reckless. Energetic, enthusiastic, curious adventurer.

### Jo Shanks
**A3 C3 E2 N2 O4**

Independent lone operator. Cool under pressure, creative and unorthodox in methods. Skilled archer.

### Captain Frye
**A4 C5 E3 N2 O2**

Loyal ship captain, follows protocol precisely. Steady, calm, traditional. By-the-book but capable.

### Wilfred Longtreader
**A4 C5 E2 N3 O4**

Loving father, meticulous long-term planner. Works in shadows, carries weight of secrets. Visionary.

---

## Antagonists

### Morbin Blackhawk
**A1 C4 E4 N2 O3**

Cruel raptor lord, dominating presence. Organized in his tyranny. Commands through fear. Coldly controlled.

### Redeye Garlackson
**A1 C3 E3 N4 O2**

Treacherous, self-serving traitor. Charming when needed but paranoid and fearful. Narrow self-interest.
